# Brand Summary — Khabar (खबर)

---

## What it is

Khabar is a Hindi-language digital news publication covering politics, national affairs, sports, business, entertainment, and technology for India's 500M+ Hindi-speaking internet users.

## Why it exists

Most Hindi news portals sacrifice readability for density — they feel like a bulletin board shouting at you. Khabar is for the reader who wants authoritative Hindi journalism that respects their intelligence and their eyes.

## Positioning

| Axis | Where Khabar sits |
|---|---|
| Loud ↔ Quiet | Slightly toward quiet — authoritative, not sensational |
| Fast ↔ Deep | Both — fast on breaking, deep on analysis |
| Generic ↔ Specific | Specific — every claim has a number or a name |
| Futuristic ↔ Grounded | Grounded — this is journalism, not a tech product |

## Personality

**Is:** Authoritative · Credible · Accessible · Sharp · Trustworthy

**Is not:** Sensational · Tabloid · Cluttered · Preachy · Elitist

## Archetype

**The Sage** — the trusted senior journalist who has seen everything, speaks plainly, cites sources, and never exaggerates.

Anti-archetype: **The Tabloid Crier** — loud, hyperbolic, optimised for outrage.

## The one-sentence promise

सही खबर, सही समय पर। *(The right news, at the right time.)*

## Taglines

- Primary: **खबर जो मायने रखती है** *(News that matters)*
- Alt 1: **सच, सरल, सटीक** *(True, Simple, Precise)*
- Alt 2: **भरोसे की आवाज़** *(The voice of trust)*

## Voice in one paragraph

Khabar writes the way a trusted editor speaks: plainly, precisely, without embellishment. Sentences are short. Claims have numbers. Readers are addressed as adults. The publication says "the minister announced" not "in a shocking development." It says "₹4,200 crore" not "thousands of crores." It reports what happened and why it matters — nothing more.
