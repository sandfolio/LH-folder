# Ads — Khabar Design System

---

## The principle

Every ad passes the **3-second hallway test**: if someone walks past a screen showing this ad, can they read the headline, understand the offer, and identify Khabar within 3 seconds?

---

| Placement | Size | Ratio |

---

## Composition — four elements, always

1. **Headline** — 1 sentence, sentence case, no hype
2. **Sub-line** — 1 sentence supporting the headline
3. **CTA** — Button: "सदस्य बनें" / "ऐप डाउनलोड करें" / "पढ़ें"
4. **Mark** — Khabar lockup or mark, always visible

---

## Backgrounds

One background type per ad. Pick one:
- `khabar-900` — authoritative, premium
- `khabar-600` — urgent, breaking context
- `paper` `#FAFAF8` — clean, editorial
- Wire photography with dark scrim overlay

Never: gradient mesh, aurora, or particle backgrounds.

---

## What's never in an ad

- Stock "diverse team around laptop" photos
- AI-generated human faces
- Exclamation marks in headlines
- Promises we can't keep ("India's #1 news source" without data)
- Countdown timers (creates false urgency)
- Animated GIFs that loop more than 3 times

---

## Paid copy voice

Cut 40% more than homepage copy. Ads are shorter than editorial:

| Surface | Max words |
|---|---|
| Instagram feed headline | 8 words |
| LinkedIn headline | 12 words |
| Google Display headline | 6 words |
| CTA button | 3 words |

---

## Short-form video (15s format)

**Structure:**
- 0–3s: Problem / hook — state the pain without voiceover if possible
- 3–10s: Solution — show Khabar in use (reading, notification)
- 10–13s: Proof — "12 लाख+ पाठक रोज़"
- 13–15s: CTA — app badge + "khabar.in"

**Production rules:**
- No stock footage of "diverse Indians on phones"
- Hindi voiceover or text-on-screen (not both simultaneously)
- Subtitles always (most mobile video watched muted)
- No background music louder than voiceover

---

## What success looks like on paid

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| CTR (Instagram) | > 1.2% |
| CTR (LinkedIn) | > 0.5% |
| Newsletter signup conversion | > 8% of ad clicks |
| App install conversion | > 12% of ad clicks |

Test one variable at a time. Never A/B test two different headlines AND two different images simultaneously.
