# Brand — Khabar (खबर)

---

## Mission

हर भारतीय को सटीक, समझने योग्य और भरोसेमंद खबर देना।  
*To give every Indian accurate, understandable, and trustworthy news.*

## Vision

भारत का सबसे विश्वसनीय हिन्दी डिजिटल समाचार मंच।  
*India's most credible Hindi digital news platform.*

## Values

| Value | What it means in practice |
|---|---|
| **सच्चाई (Truth)** | We verify before we publish. We correct when wrong. |
| **स्पष्टता (Clarity)** | Plain language. No jargon. Readable at class 8 level. |
| **तेज़ी (Speed)** | Breaking news within minutes — but accuracy is never sacrificed for speed. |
| **निष्पक्षता (Fairness)** | All sides of a story, when sides exist. |
| **सम्मान (Respect)** | Readers are adults. We do not sensationalise their grief or their politics. |

---

## Positioning statement

For Hindi-speaking Indians who want news they can trust, Khabar is the digital news platform that delivers accurate, fast, and clear journalism — unlike competitors who prioritise outrage and clicks, Khabar reports what happened and why it matters.

---

## Target audience

**Primary:** Hindi-speaking urban and semi-urban Indians, 22–45, active on mobile, consume news in the mornings and evenings, follow politics, cricket, and regional affairs, distrust sensationalism, share articles on WhatsApp.

**Secondary:** Hindi diaspora outside India (Gulf, UK, USA) who want to stay connected with home.

---

## Audience insight

The Hindi news reader has been conditioned by decades of sensational, loud, breaking-news-everything journalism. They are simultaneously addicted to the format and deeply cynical about it. Khabar wins by being the publication they *recommend* to others — not just the one they consume themselves.

---

## Archetype

**The Sage** — wise, calm, authoritative. Speaks from experience, not from emotion. States facts. Earns trust over time.

**Anti-archetype:** The Town Crier — loud, urgent, every story is the biggest story ever.
